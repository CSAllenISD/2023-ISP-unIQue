Feeling Depressed, Down, or Hopeless?

Depression can often be a tricky emotion to navigate. Maybe you're lacking energy or struggling to find motivation to do anything. Whatever you may be feeling, know that it is completely valid and there are things that can help.


Take a minute to sit with whatever you're feeling. This may feel unpleasent, but acknowledging and accepting how you feel is the first step to feeling better. Additionally, try identifing the source of the emotion and why you're feeling that way.


Next, try talking to a friend or family member. Know that there are people around you that can help. Whether that be processing the emotion and working through it, or just being there for you.


It might be hard, but if you can, try doing some small self-care things for yourself, such as eating a snack, taking a shower, or doing a small chore for the sense of accomplishment. Make sure to get plenty of rest and take care of yourself.


We know somtimes it can be hard to find the motivation to keep going, and progress may seem slow, but take the small victories, whether it be getting out of bed or drinking a cup of water, and know that you can get through this.
