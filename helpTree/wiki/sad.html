Feeling Depressed

Depression can often be a tricky emotion to navigate. Maybe you're lacking energy or struggling to find motivation to do anything. Whatever you may be feeling, know that it is completely valid and there are things that can help.


Take a minute to sit with the emotion. This may feel unpleasent, but acknowledging and accepting how you feel is the first step to feeling better. Additionally, try identifing the source of the emotion and why you're feeling that way.


Next, try talking to a friend or family member. Know that there are people around you that can help. Whether that be processing the emotion and working through it, or just being there for you.

Sadness can make you feel helpless and alone, and it can be hard to navigate. To start, know that your emotions are completely valid and it's totally okay to feel sad.


The first step to feeling better is accepting your emotions, rather than supressing what you're feeling. Try identifying the source of your emotion, and understand why it's making you feel that way.


Next, try talking to a close friend or family member. You are not a burden for asking for help from those who love you. Talking to someone can help you process your situation, or gain a fresh perspective on it.


Even though it can be hard, it's also extremely important to continue to take care of yourself. Get enough rest and do things you enjoy. Making sure your body is healthy can be enough to make you feel emotionally rejuvenated.
